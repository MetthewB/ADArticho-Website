Sources

Explore the references and resources that supported our project.

Datasets

  • New Yorker Caption Contest (NYCC):

    Data sourced from The New Yorker archives, containing captions submitted to the weekly contest.

  • Oxford Humor in Context (OHIC):

    Data sourced from the Oxford University Research Archive, containing approximately 2.9 million humorous image-text pairs.

Tools and Libraries

  • Python: Used for data preprocessing, analysis, and modeling.
  • NLTK, TextBlob, VADER: Libraries for linguistic and sentiment analysis.
  • scikit-learn: Used for statistical modeling and machine learning.
  • Transformers (Hugging Face): Used for generating embeddings and zero-shot classification.
  • Matplotlib, Seaborn: Libraries for data visualization.
